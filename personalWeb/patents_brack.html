Bracket for Welding the Circuit Board
Patent No.201020254015.8. First inventor

On the welding circuit board, fix electronic components on obverse side of the board while adding soldering tin welding on the verso of the board. At that moment, the board is easy to cause loose welding because the electronic components will slip down due to its gravitational effects. In addition, because of the small size of most electronic components, magnifier is needed during welding. But there is no fixed place for it in the traditional laboratory. So itâ€™s inconvenient to tidy the test bench and use the magnifier.

This invention displays a bracket platform with pedestal, which contains a magnetic tray, a shelf for supporting the board and a magnifier. The bracket platform, which can fix the circuit board efficiently, combine the magnifier, magnetic tray and welding-searing-iron on the platform and finish the whole experiment progress from planting the components to welding fluently, brings great convenience, enhances the comfort and improves efficiency.
