Well-proportioned Daub Machine Used in Couplant of Medical Ultrasonography Probe. Patent No.201020280451.2. First inventor

Couplant is necessary in Medical Ultrasonography Probe. Due to the principle of the Ultrasound of Color Doppler, the couplant was required to realize the close combination of the probes and the parts tested. In present technologies, the couplants and ultrasound probes are apart, so doctors have to test after squeezing the couplants on the surfaces of the probes or the receivers according to their experiment and they have to do it with one hand to take the couplant can while the other hand with the probe at the beginning of the testing. In addition, the quantity of the couplants depends on the gnosis of the doctors, so it is easy to cause the wasting of the couplant which is more expensive than medical equipments. This invention displays a kind of even daub machine for couplants in medical color doppler ultrasound. And it contains power plugs, daub sprinkler parts, tiny pump and can carrier which contains couplants can and connecting part.

The daub sprinkler parts contain daub sprinkler and handle. The daub sprinkler contains connector and sprinkler and the bottoms of connector and sprinkler combined in mixed air container which has an air tube, one side of which connected with a tube-line and a micro-pump while the other with a jet orifice which is connected with mixed air container. The connector is combined with liquid outlets. And daub the liquid on the surface of skin with Bernouili incoming principle. Compared with the modern technology, the advantages of this practical new one are below: 1. decrease the work of the doctors and increase the work efficiency by auto-control the couplantsâ€™ usage; 2. reduce the use of couplant as more as possible under the same imaging effect; 3. enhance the fitness of the patients and reduce the materials of cleaning the couplants.
